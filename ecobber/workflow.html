Our Sustainable Workflow

At Ecobber, sustainability is woven into every step of our manufacturing process. We continuously innovate to minimize environmental impact and maximize resource efficiency.

Zero-Waste Production

We implement advanced techniques to ensure that almost no material goes to waste during our production cycle. From raw material input to final product, every byproduct is either reused or recycled.

Closed-Loop Manufacturing

  • Utilizing advanced rubber compounding techniques
  • Minimizing scrap and defective products through precise process control
  • Repurposing excess materials for new product lines

Renewable Energy Sources

Powering our operations with clean energy is fundamental to our environmental commitment. We invest in on-site renewable solutions and procure energy from certified green providers.

Harnessing Clean Power

  • On-site solar power generation for operations
  • Sourcing renewable energy certificates for off-site power
  • Implementing energy-efficient machinery to reduce overall consumption

Water Recycling Systems

Water is a precious resource, and our state-of-the-art recycling systems ensure its responsible use. We treat and reuse process water, significantly reducing our freshwater intake.

Advanced Water Treatment

  • Closed-loop water cooling systems for machinery
  • Biological and physical filtration for wastewater treatment
  • Monitoring and optimization of water usage across all processes

Sustainable Sourcing Practices

Our commitment to sustainability begins with the raw materials. We meticulously select suppliers who share our values for ethical production, environmental protection, and social responsibility.

Ethical and Eco-Conscious Materials

  • Prioritizing natural rubber from certified sustainable plantations
  • Auditing suppliers for environmental and social compliance
  • Investing in research for new sustainable rubber formulations

From Product to Sub-product

Our commitment to zero waste extends beyond the initial manufacturing. We have developed a unique process to transform end-of-life products and production scraps into valuable sub-products.

Creating Value from Waste

Instead of discarding defective or used rubber items, we process them through a specialized granulation and molding system. This allows us to create entirely new product lines, such as rubber mats and flooring, closing the loop completely.
